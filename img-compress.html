How to Use

  1. Upload an Image: Click the "Choose Image" button or drag and drop your image into the upload area.
  2. Adjust Quality: Use the slider to set your desired compression quality.
  3. Compress: Click the "Compress Image" button to process your image.
  4. Preview & Download: View the original and compressed images with their file sizes. Click "Download" to save the compressed image.

Troubleshooting

  • If the image does not display, ensure that you have selected a valid image file.
  • If compression fails, try reducing the compression quality or using a different image format.
  • For performance issues, verify your system resources and file size; larger files may take longer to process.
  • If the download button is not showing, confirm that the compression process has completed successfully.
